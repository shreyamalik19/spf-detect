Do I need SPF today?


What is SPF?

SPF stands for sun protection factor.

Higher SPF = better protection against UV (ultraviolet) rays.

SPF 50 allows one-fiftieth (2%) of UV rays to reach your skin.

Benefits of SPF

The key benefits of SPF are UV ray protection, reduced risks of skin cancer, and significant reduction of premature aging.

SPF is the single most important step in a daily skincare routine.
